Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy describes what happens to information when you visit facultyguard.com. It's written in plain language, and it reflects exactly what this site does today — no more, no less.

What this site collects

Faculty Guard's website does not run analytics, tracking pixels, or cookies of its own, and it does not have a contact form, account system, or checkout that stores your information on our servers.

When you click a "Get Essentials," "Get the Full System," or similar button, it opens a pre-filled email in your own email application, addressed to intake@facultyguard.com. Nothing is sent or stored until you personally choose to send that email. Any information in that email (your name, email address, and message) is handled the same way as any other email you send us, and is used only to respond to your inquiry and fulfill a purchase.

Third-party services

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com / fonts.gstatic.com). Loading a webpage's fonts from Google's servers means your browser makes a request directly to Google, which may log standard technical information such as your IP address, in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. We don't control or receive that data.

We do not use any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services on this site at this time.

Email and purchase information

If you email us to purchase or inquire about a Faculty Guard product, we use the information you provide (such as your name, institutional affiliation, and email address) solely to deliver the product or service you requested, respond to your questions, and maintain basic business records. We do not sell or rent your information to third parties.

Your choices

Since this site itself doesn't collect or store personal data, there's nothing to opt out of on the site. For any information you've sent us by email, you can ask us at any time to delete our copy of that correspondence by emailing intake@facultyguard.com.

Changes to this policy

If how this site handles information changes — for example, if we add a checkout system, analytics, or a contact form in the future — we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to intake@facultyguard.com.

This page is a general-purpose privacy notice reflecting the site's current, simple technical setup. It is not a substitute for legal advice — if Faculty Guard's data practices expand (payment processing, a contact form, marketing email, etc.), this policy should be reviewed and updated by qualified legal counsel before those changes go live.
