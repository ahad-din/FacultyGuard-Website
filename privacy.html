Privacy

Privacy Policy

FacultyGuard collects only the information needed to respond to inquiries and support agreed faculty partnerships.

Information Collected

FacultyGuard may collect contact details, faculty role, package or FG System interests, inquiry content, and materials voluntarily provided during an engagement.

Use of Information

Information is used to respond to inquiries, assess fit, provide agreed services, maintain business records, and improve FacultyGuard operations.

Student and Course Information

Do not submit student names, identification numbers, grades, or unnecessary personally identifiable student information. FacultyGuard is not an institutional records repository.

Data Sharing

FacultyGuard does not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers necessary to operate the website and manage communications, subject to their applicable terms.

Retention and Requests

Information is retained only as reasonably necessary for inquiry, service, legal, and business purposes. Privacy questions or requests may be sent to intake@facultyguard.com.
