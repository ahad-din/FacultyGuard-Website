FacultyGuard FAQ

Is Faculty Guard an AI grading tool?

No. Faculty Guard does not replace instructor judgment or grade student work. It provides reusable faculty systems, templates, checklists, and documentation workflows that help instructors manage grading, academic integrity, AI policy, and communication more consistently.

Who is Faculty Guard designed for?

Faculty Guard is designed for higher education instructors, online faculty, adjuncts, department coordinators, and professors managing grading volume, academic integrity concerns, AI policy questions, and documentation demands.

Can Faculty Guard help with AI syllabus policies?

Yes. Faculty Guard includes AI policy language, student-facing guidance, disclosure options, and assignment-level language instructors can adapt to their courses.

Does Faculty Guard work with Canvas, D2L, Blackboard, or other LMS platforms?

Yes. Faculty Guard is LMS-neutral. The systems can be adapted for Canvas, D2L Brightspace, Blackboard, Moodle, and other course platforms.

Is this for individual faculty or departments?

Faculty Guard currently supports individual instructors through downloadable systems and limited professional review. Departmental and institutional support may be available by request.
